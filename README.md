 ## Project Overview:
 This project analyzes Food Tech Domain Zomato business performance including revenue, city performance, and restaurant-level insights.
 
 ## Business Objective:
 To analyze Year-Over-Year Percentage of Restaurants Onboarding,Online Delivery Penetration,Table Booking adoption,and city-wise performance to improve business decision-making

 ## Key KPIs
 
 1.Restaurant Supply Count by (Year, Month, Quater)
 2.Supply Density by City
 3.Rating Distribution
 4.Price Band Mix
 5.Online Delivery Penetration
 6.Table booking adoption
 7.Cusine mix index

 ## Insights Generated 
  1. Identified top revenue-generating cities.
  2. Analyzed monthly,yearly,quaterly growth trends.
  3. Detected high-performing restaurant categories.
     
## Business Impact The dashboard helps stakeholders: - 
1.Optimize restaurant partnerships 
2.Track performance across cities.
3.Avaoiding Single Cuisine Domainance.
